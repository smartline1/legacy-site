]]> From armchair exercise and community acupuncture sessions, lessons in first aid and how to use the internet safely, to providing a green space for a local community, Smartline is transforming the lives of people in Camborne, Pool, and Redruth.

To help shape our future community plans we want to hear about activities you would like to take part in over the next 12 months of the project.

Please help us by completing this feedback form: click here.

Among the successful projects helping to bring local communities together are monthly coffee mornings and exercise classes in the Wesley Chapel, here’s what some of the attendees thought:

“We wanted to find out what was happening in the local area and to make friends” said Shirley Roberts of the coffee mornings. “Coming to the coffee mornings has been a fantastic way of doing this and we are so grateful to Smartline for organising them”.

Among those taking part in the exercise classes is 81 year old Joan Hampton. Joan had recently moved into a retirement complex and was worried about “ending up just sitting in a chair and not moving” when she heard about the classes through her church and decided to give them a try.   “I can feel myself becoming fitter and stronger“ she said. “Everyone in the class is very friendly and we all have a laugh and lots of fun”.
