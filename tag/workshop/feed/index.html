]]> Sound mapping workshop, 29 August 2019

Smartline is supporting Falmouth-based organisation FoAM Kernow with the development of the sonic kayak – a project to enable blind and partially sighted people to enjoy Cornwall’s waterways.

FoAM is looking for people with visual impairments to help them to create a sound map of a lake in Cornwall. The map can be used by people with sight problems to navigate the lake.
