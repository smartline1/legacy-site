Free Business Workshop: Evaluate your wellbeing impact

13 June and 4 July 2019, Penventon Park Hotel, Redruth

Join us to work with University of Exeter academics in The Penventon Park Hotel for a FREE two-day workshop designed to support enterprises and practitioners to evaluate and manage the wellbeing difference you make. Learn how to use powerful data to evidence your impact.

These workshops are recommended for enterprises that aim to make a positive difference to their clients’, patients’, or customers’ health and wellbeing.

Workshop Day 1: Thursday 13th June 9am – 3pm

Workshop Day 2: Thursday 4th July 9am – 3pm

Following both workshops, you will be able to:

  • Plan the most appropriate evaluation approach for your enterprise (qualitative, quantitative, small scale, large scale).
  • Identify the most appropriate, reliable and validated questionnaire for your enterprise, and how to use them.
  • Legally and ethically collect and store the data.
  • Analyse and interpret the data to help you develop and promote your enterprise.

Lunch is provided.
