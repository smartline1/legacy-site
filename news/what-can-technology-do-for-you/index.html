What can technology do for you?

On Wednesday 31st October we are hosting an event with our friends at EPIC exclusively for residents of Coastline Housing.

The event will show how technology can improve everyday lives, from contacting friends, using VR for relaxation or robotics to help with exercise. Learn how to safely shop online, check out library books using your tablet, test out virtual reality and meet our robotic pets – and much more!

Whether you are already a savvy silver surfer or are new to the digital world entirely we will have something for everyone to enjoy and learn on the day.

On the day you can:

•Learn how to use your tablet to really make your life easier, based on what you need

• Join in on a sit down exercise class to get led by Pepper

• Meet and greet our companion pets

• Explore a different location, without leaving your chair using Virtual Reality

• Learn how to keep connected with your friends and family using tablets and Skype

The event will take place at Miners Court in Redruth on 31st October between 10:30am – 3:30pm. If you have a tablet / iPad / Smart Phone please these along so you can join in on your own device.

This event is not open to the general public but if you are a Coastline customer and would like to attend please contact Donna at Miners Court or email EPIC@plymouth.ac.uk
