Innovation and e-health – Knowledge exchange for business delivery

Innovation and e-health – Knowledge exchange for business delivery

Research leads: Professor Gareth Shaw and Stephen Hickman

The aim of this research theme is to facilitate both the exchange and absorption of knowledge of business opportunities relating to e-health in SMEs using the process of co-creation workshops. This aim is researched by two key objectives:

  • To increase investment in research and innovation by SMEs in sectors and technologies identified by Smartline.
  • Increase the number of SMEs engaged in knowledge exchange, collaboration and innovation with research institutions working with e-Health products.
